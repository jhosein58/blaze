# 🔥 Blaze AI Platform

A modern, next-generation AI platform designed to provide fast, secure, and transparent access to the world's leading AI models through a unified API.

## ✨ Vision

Blaze is built around a simple idea:

> AI should be accessible, transparent, and privacy-first.

Instead of locking users into a single provider, Blaze connects you to a wide range of state-of-the-art AI models while keeping the platform open and easy to understand.

## 🔒 Privacy & Transparency

Privacy is the foundation of Blaze.

- Open source by design
- Fully transparent codebase
- No hidden data processing
- Users can inspect exactly how their data is handled

We believe trust comes from transparency—not promises.

## 🚀 Goals

- Simple access to modern AI technologies
- High performance
- Strong security
- Clean and scalable architecture
- A great developer experience
